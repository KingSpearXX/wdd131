Cavite, Philippines

Cavite is a province in the Philippines located in the CALABARZON region on the island of Luzon. It is situated just south of Metro Manila, making it part of the Greater Manila Area. Known for its rich history, Cavite played a significant role in the Philippine Revolution against Spanish colonial rule. The Declaration of Philippine Independence was proclaimed in Kawit, Cavite, on June 12, 1898.
